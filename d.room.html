Deluxe Room



Room's details :

Our Deluxe Rooms are spacious havens of comfort, with fantastic pool or garden views, adorned with earthy tones for a warm & cozy atmosphere. These ultra-modern rooms have an average size of 55 sqm with a separate sitting area for your comfort & convenience. Each Deluxe Room is equipped with a work desk, flat-screen TV, and a wide variety of local and international channels. In-room controlled air conditioning is very convenient during warmer summer months. Room service is available 24 hours a day and a well-stocked minibar fridge is supplied for your enjoyment. Our Deluxe Rooms are spacious enough to accommodate a maximum of two adults and one child. Baby cots and extra beds are available on request, as are smoking and non-smoking rooms. Bathrooms feature a combined shower and bathtub, double vanity sinks, a hairdryer, a makeup mirror, plush towels, and bathrobes. Guests can also enjoy complimentary access to the fitness center.
